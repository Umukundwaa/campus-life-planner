About Campus Life Planner

Empowering Students to Stay Organized and Motivated

🌟 Our Mission

Campus Life Planner helps students balance their academic, personal, and social lives. With features like weekly trackers, task management, and engagement statistics, it brings structure and clarity to your daily routine.

👩‍💻 Meet the Developer

This project was designed and developed by Ange Umukundwa — a passionate student who loves technology, problem-solving, and innovation. The goal is to inspire others, especially women, to explore technology with confidence.
